About Aifuyi 爱弗亿

Who We Are

Yuhuan Aifuyi Technology Co., Ltd. (formerly known as Yuhuan Aifuyi Machinery Factory), founded in 2007 and located in Taizhou City, Zhejiang Province, is an enterprise mainly engaged in research and experimental development.

The registered capital of the enterprise is 1.2 million RMB, with the paid-in capital of 1.2 million RMB.

General Projects

R&D of auto parts; R&D of motorcycles and their parts; R&D of hardware products; software development; manufacturing of auto parts, motorcycle parts and fasteners. (Except for projects that must be approved in accordance with the law, the enterprise may independently carry out business activities in accordance with the law with its business license.)
